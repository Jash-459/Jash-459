### 💫 About Me:
🔭 I'm Jash Hamirani, a DevSecOps Engineer at Zigram Data Technologies, passionate about bridging the gap between development, security, and operations — building systems that are not just scalable and reliable, but secure by design.

🛡️ My expertise spans AWS, Azure, GCP, Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, Ansible, Jenkins, Helm, ArgoCD, OpenTelemetry, Grafana, and the broader cloud-native ecosystem — with a strong emphasis on shifting security left.

🤝 I'm continuously expanding my knowledge across software engineering, infrastructure automation, distributed systems, Linux internals, and the ever-evolving landscape of DevSecOps practices.

🌱 I work with Python, Bash, Django, PostgreSQL, REST APIs, and modern CI/CD pipelines to design and deliver solutions that don't just ship fast — they ship safe.

💬 Feel free to ask me about cloud architecture, DevSecOps, infrastructure-as-code, security automation, system design, competitive programming, or navigating a career in tech.
